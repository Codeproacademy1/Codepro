About the Course

Scratch is a free, block-based programming tool developed by MIT. It introduces essential coding concepts, allowing students to create animations, games, and interactive stories in a playful environment.

Our Scratch course at Code Pro Academy is designed for children and teens (ages 6–14). By focusing on visual blocks, we help students understand logical thinking, algorithmic thinking, and problem-solving in an engaging way.

Why Scratch?

Scratch is perfect for beginners because it eliminates the hurdle of syntax errors. Drag-and-drop blocks encourage creativity, collaboration, and quick learning without frustration.

  • Highly visual, intuitive interface
  • Strong educational community and resources
  • Encourages experimentation and design thinking
