ðŸ“¡ Special Pins

Pin Function
30 RUN - Reset the Pico W (connect to ground to reset)
GPIO24 & GPIO25 Internal use (Wi-Fi chip & onboard LED)

Important Note: GPIO23, GPIO24, and GPIO25 are used for the onboard Wi-Fi chip and LED. Avoid using these pins in your projects to prevent interference with the wireless functionality.
